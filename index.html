3DTracker


See also a video. 動画はこちら

We developed a novel 3D marker-less motion capture and behavior analysis system for interacting rodents, named 3DTracker. It's open-source software available at PLoS ONE. I am preparing to provide updates of the software through Github.

For details of the program, please see the paper below.

Matsumoto J, Urakawa S, Takamura Y, Malcher-Lopes R, Hori E, Tomaz C, Ono T, and Nishijo H (2013) A 3D-video-based computerized analysis of social and sexual interactions in rats. PLoS ONE 8:e78460 - Download

Questions, suggestions or comments about the software is welcome in this discussion forum.
